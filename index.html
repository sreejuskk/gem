About Us

Galway East Malayali Cultural Community (GEM) registered with Galway County Public participation Network (Reg.GALPPN-01679), is a non-profitable cultural, voluntary organization comprising of almost 170 members including children. The community celebrates both Indian and Irish cultural events. The events are mostly conducted in the Mervue Community Centre, Galway. The community also involves in lot more social activities to help both Irish and Indian society. We operate based on a Bylaw approved by the executive committee of GEM. As a community dedicated to preserving and promoting unity, love, care and compassion, this event aims to bring together diverse backgrounds to celebrate and share our rich culture.

What We Do

Cultural Exchange

We host a variety of events throughout the year celebrating the traditions of both India and Ireland. Most of our gatherings are held at the Mervue Community Centre, creating a welcoming hub for social interaction.

Social Responsibility

Beyond cultural festivities, GEM is deeply involved in charitable initiatives and social activities aimed at supporting the wider community in both nations.

Community Integration

We strive to create an inclusive environment where individuals from diverse backgrounds can come together to share experiences, celebrate heritage, and build lasting friendships.
